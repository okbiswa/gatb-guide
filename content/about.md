# About Me

Hello! I'm **Biswarup Nandi**, the creator of GAT-B Guide.

## Why GAT-B Guide?

As a GAT-B aspirant myself, I know how overwhelming the admission process can be — dozens of institutes, varying cutoffs, different categories, and limited guidance. I built GAT-B Guide to make this journey easier for every candidate.

## What Does GAT-B Guide Do?

GAT-B Guide uses **historical cutoff data from 2021–2025** to help you:

- Discover institutes that match your GAT-B score
- Understand your chances at different programmes
- Explore 83+ participating institutes across India
- Find premier institutions worth applying to

## The Technology

GAT-B Guide is built with modern web technologies:

- **Next.js** for fast, server-rendered pages
- **TypeScript** for reliable code
- **Tailwind CSS** for beautiful design
- Deployed on **Vercel** for global performance
