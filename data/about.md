# About GATBVerse

GATBVerse was created to solve a problem faced by thousands of GAT-B aspirants every year.

After results are announced, students often struggle to identify:

- Which institutes they are eligible for
- Which programmes are available
- Which applications are currently open
- Which institutes match their scores

Information is frequently scattered across multiple websites, admission portals, PDFs, and notifications.

GATBVerse aims to simplify this process by bringing institute information, historical cutoff trends, programme details, and admission opportunities together in a single platform.

## Mission

To help biotechnology aspirants make informed admission decisions through accessible and data-driven guidance.

## Features

- Historical cutoff analysis
- Institute discovery
- Programme exploration
- Admission opportunity recommendations
- Educational resources and guidance
