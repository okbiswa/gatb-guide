## Mission

## Why I Built GAT-B Guide
I was once a student searching through hundreds of PDFs, university websites, and YouTube videos just to understand where I could apply after GAT-B. I realized thousands of students repeat the same exhausting process every year. GAT-B Guide is my attempt to make that journey simpler, faster, and much more transparent. Instead of creating another static website, I decided to build an AI-powered admission mentor that reasons like a senior student or academic advisor.
