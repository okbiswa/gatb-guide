# Institute Research Profiles (Top 25)

## 1. Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)
**Location:** New Delhi, Delhi
**Seats:** ~200 across programs
**Overview:** Renowned as India’s premier university in life sciences. The School of Life Sciences (SLS) offers interdisciplinary research from molecular biology to ecology.
**Key Areas:** Genetics, cell biology, and environmental biology.
**Notable Faculty:** Prof. Asis Datta, Prof. MRS Rao.

## 2. IIT Indore (MF School of Biosciences & Bioengineering)
**Location:** Indore, Madhya Pradesh
**Seats:** ~30 seats
**Overview:** A newer IIT program focusing on interdisciplinary biotech and biomedical engineering. Offers a 5-year integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D. and 2-year M.Sc. Biotech.
**Key Areas:** Structural biology, molecular immunology, disease diagnostics, computational biology (e.g., Dr. Amit Kumar’s NMR lab), protein modeling, biomaterials, systems biology.

## 3. University of Hyderabad (UoH)
**Location:** Hyderabad, Telangana
**Seats:** ~40 seats
**Overview:** Its School of Life Sciences (est. 1977) is a multidisciplinary hub covering plant sciences, animal biology, biochemistry, and newer fields like bioinformatics. Receives heavy DBT/DST funding.
**Key Areas:** Molecular genetics, neuroscience, cell biology, immunology, microbiology.

## 4. Regional Centre for Biotechnology (RCB)
**Location:** Faridabad, Haryana
**Seats:** ~25 seats
**Overview:** A DBT-established national institute (UNESCO Category-II) combining biotech with engineering and medical research. Offers an integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D. program.
**Key Areas:** Computational biology, disease biology, translational biotech. Facilities include NMR, EM, BSL-3 labs. Emphasizes multi-disciplinary projects at the biotech-engineering interface.

## 5. RGCB (Rajiv Gandhi Centre for Biotechnology)
**Location:** Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala
**Seats:** ~30 seats
**Overview:** A DBT-funded research institute devoted to molecular biology and biotech. Runs an M.Sc. Biotechnology program affiliated with University of Kerala. Has a regional DNA fingerprinting center.
**Key Areas:** Biomedical research in cancer, infectious diseases, cardiovascular/metabolic disorders, regenerative medicine, plant disease biology.

## 6. Banaras Hindu University (BHU)
**Location:** Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
**Seats:** ~50 seats
**Overview:** One of India’s oldest universities. BHU’s Institute of Science houses biotechnology research. Notable centers in Microbiology, Botany, and Bioinformatics.
**Key Areas:** Microbial biotechnology, animal biotechnology, plant genetic engineering.

## 7. Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU)
**Location:** Pune, Maharashtra
**Seats:** ~25 seats
**Overview:** A major state university with strong biotech and microbiology departments. Has collaborations with CSIR labs. Offers M.Sc. Biotech and M.Tech Biotech.
**Key Areas:** Environmental biotechnology, microbiome research, medical biotech. Featured labs: Environmental Biotech Lab, Cell Biology Unit.

## 8. Pondicherry University
**Location:** Puducherry
**Seats:** ~15 seats
**Overview:** Central University with DBT PG biotech. The School of Life Sciences drives research. Notable lab: Rice Biotechnology Centre. Offers integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D. and M.Sc. programs.
**Key Areas:** Marine biotechnology, plant-microbe interactions, computational biology.

## 9. NIT Durgapur
**Location:** Durgapur, West Bengal
**Seats:** ~20 seats
**Overview:** National Institute of Technology with strong applied biotech programs. Well-funded by DST/CSIR. Offers M.Sc. Biotechnology and MBEC.
**Key Areas:** Industrial biotech, bioinformatics, genetic engineering.

## 10. PGIMER Chandigarh
**Location:** Chandigarh
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** A premier medical institute; DBT funds its M.Sc. Biotechnology program. Known for clinical applications and translational research.
**Key Areas:** Medical biotechnology, genomics, pharmacology.

## 11. Alagappa University
**Location:** Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** State university known for science programs. Has a Centre of Excellence in Biotechnology. Alumni often pursue research in biotech industries.
**Key Areas:** Microbial biotechnology, immunology, plant biotech.

## 12. Amity University, Gurugram
**Location:** Gurugram, Haryana
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** Private university with DBT PG biotech. Modern labs and industry links.
**Key Areas:** Bioinformatics, genetic engineering, pharmaceuticals.

## 13. Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI)
**Location:** New Delhi
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** Central University known for biotechnology and pharmaceutical sciences. M.Sc. Biotech opened in 2024.
**Key Areas:** Molecular biology, drug design, reproductive health.

## 14. Institute of Advanced Research (IAR)
**Location:** Sola, Gujarat
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** Private institute focusing on biotech and research. Ties with DBT/CMI for postgrad programs.
**Key Areas:** Plant biotechnology, bioprocessing.

## 15. Banasthali Vidyapith
**Location:** Tonk, Rajasthan
**Seats:** ~30 seats (women only)
**Overview:** Renowned women’s university. Strong in agro-bio and computational biology.
**Key Areas:** Plant sciences, environmental biotech.

## 16. University of Delhi (North Campus)
**Location:** Delhi
**Seats:** ~20 seats
**Overview:** Renowned labs like the Plant Biotechnology Lab. Offers M.Sc. in Biotechnology and Genetics.
**Key Areas:** Molecular genetics, plant biology, structural biology.

## 17. Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda
**Location:** Vadodara, Gujarat
**Seats:** ~15 seats
**Overview:** Multidisciplinary university housing a national genome laboratory and tissue culture facilities.
**Key Areas:** Industrial biotech, biochemical engineering, genetics.

## 18. Assam Agricultural University (AAU)
**Location:** Jorhat, Assam
**Seats:** ~11 seats
**Overview:** The Rajiv Gandhi School of Biotechnology is notable. Focus on agricultural and animal biotechnology.
**Key Areas:** Crop biotechnology, fermentation, natural products.

## 19. Cotton University
**Location:** Guwahati, Assam
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** Formerly Cotton College. Offers MSc Biotech and Life Sciences.
**Key Areas:** Biodiversity, medicinal plants, computational genomics.

## 20. Guru Jambheshwar Univ. of Science & Technology
**Location:** Hisar, Haryana
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** State university with biotech programs.
**Key Areas:** Plant biotechnology, biofuels.

## 21. Guru Nanak Dev University
**Location:** Amritsar, Punjab
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** Known for Department of Bioengineering.
**Key Areas:** Microbial biotech, environmental biotech, food technology.

## 22. IIS (Deemed Univ.), Jaipur
**Location:** Jaipur, Rajasthan
**Seats:** ~10 seats (women only)
**Overview:** Research in biomedical sciences and women’s health.

## 23. Jamia Hamdard
**Location:** New Delhi
**Seats:** ~15 seats
**Overview:** Deemed University with strong pharma and biotech departments.
**Key Areas:** Drug design, molecular biology, traditional medicinal plants.

## 24. University of Kerala
**Location:** Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala
**Seats:** ~20 seats
**Overview:** State university affiliated to RGCB.
**Key Areas:** Marine biotechnology, plant genomics, bioinformatics.

## 25. University of Kashmir
**Location:** Srinagar, J&K
**Seats:** ~10 seats
**Overview:** State University.
**Key Areas:** Plant molecular biology, water quality, biotechnology applications in Himalayan flora.
