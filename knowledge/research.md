# Biotechnology Research & Careers

## Research Domains in India
Biotechnology is a vast field. Major research domains in India include:
- **Molecular Biology & Genetics**: Focuses on gene expression, heredity, and cellular mechanisms.
- **Bioinformatics & Computational Biology**: Combines biology, computer science, and data analysis to understand biological data (e.g., genomics, proteomics).
- **Disease Biology & Cancer Biology**: Investigates the mechanisms of human diseases, oncology, and therapeutic interventions.
- **Plant & Agricultural Biotechnology**: Focuses on crop improvement, plant genetics, and agricultural sustainability.
- **Industrial & Environmental Biotechnology**: Focuses on bioprocess engineering, fermentation, and using microbes for environmental cleanup.

## Higher Studies
Many M.Sc. Biotechnology graduates pursue a Ph.D. either in India (at premier institutes like IISc, TIFR, IITs, JNU, RCB, NII, IGIB) or abroad. Funding for Ph.D. in India is generally secured through national exams like CSIR-NET, DBT-JRF, or GATE.

## Biotechnology Careers
Beyond academia, biotech professionals work in:
- **Pharmaceuticals & Drug Discovery**: R&D for new therapeutics.
- **Data Science & Bioinformatics**: Analyzing large-scale omics data.
- **Clinical Research**: Managing and monitoring clinical trials.
- **Scientific Publishing & EdTech**: Writing, reviewing, and building tools for scientific education.
