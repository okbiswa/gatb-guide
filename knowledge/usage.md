# How to Use GAT-B Guide

## Step-by-Step Usage Guide

### 1. Enter Your Details
On the homepage, enter your exact GAT-B score (a number). Select your reservation category (UR, OBC-NCL, SC, ST, EWS). 

### 2. Choose Preferences
Use the filters to select your preferred course type (e.g., M.Sc. Biotechnology, Integrated MS-PhD, M.Tech). You can also type in a preferred state or specific research interest to narrow down your options.

### 3. Interpret Recommendations
Click "Find Institutes". The system will process your score against historical cutoff data.
- **Top Matches**: These are colleges where your score is safely above last year's cutoff. You should definitely include these in your preference list.
- **Ambitious Choices**: These are colleges where your score is slightly below the cutoff. It is risky but possible. Always add a few ambitious choices at the top of your counseling list, followed by safe matches.

### 4. Explore Institutes
Click "View Details" on any recommended institute. This opens a dedicated page showing their research specializations, top laboratories, eligibility criteria, and a detailed seat matrix.

### 5. Watch Discussions
We provide links to verified YouTube discussions and counseling sessions for many institutes. Click the YouTube links on the institute cards to hear from current students and professors.

### 6. Use the AI Advisor
At any time, you can click the floating "Ask AI Admission Advisor" button in the bottom right. You can chat naturally with our Gemini-powered assistant. 
- Ask it to compare two colleges.
- Ask it to exclude certain regions.
- Ask it to explain why a college was recommended as "Ambitious".
The AI uses the exact same data to give you personalized mentorship.
